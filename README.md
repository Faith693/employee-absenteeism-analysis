# 📊 Employee Absenteeism Analysis

## 🧩 Business Problem
Employee absenteeism leads to productivity loss and increased operational costs. This project analyzes absenteeism patterns and associated costs to help organizations reduce employee absence and improve workforce efficiency.

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Absenteeism hours
- Absence reasons
- Employee demographics (age, gender)
- Department and job role
- Overtime data
- Location/branch information

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard and visualization)
- Excel / SQL (Data preparation and analysis)

## 🔍 Key Insights
- High absenteeism hours significantly impact organizational costs
- Certain absence reasons (e.g., maternity leave, illness) contribute the most to total absence
- Absenteeism varies across departments and locations
- Higher absenteeism is associated with lower employee performance
- Trends show fluctuations in absenteeism over time

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Implement targeted wellness and health programs
- Monitor high-absence departments and roles
- Improve workforce planning to manage absenteeism impact
- Introduce policies to reduce avoidable absences

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This analysis highlights key absenteeism drivers and provides actionable insights to reduce costs and improve workforce productivity.
